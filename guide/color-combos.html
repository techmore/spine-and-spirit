Color Combination Cheat Sheet

Curated color palettes for Bible rebinds. Each combo specifies leather, edge treatment, ribbons, thread, and headbands. These are designed to look cohesive and premium — the combinations the top rebinders actually use.

Golden rule: Pick 1 hero color for the leather, 1-2 accent colors for ribbons/edges, and 1-2 neutral ribbons to balance. Never use more than 4 ribbon colors. When in doubt, include cream or gold — they work with everything.

Combination Principles

Use these rules to build your own combos:

  1. Warm leathers (brown, tan, cognac, chestnut, oxblood) → warm ribbons (gold, cream, burgundy, sage) → gold edges. This is the classic formula.
  2. Cool leathers (gray, navy, black, forest green) → cool or contrasting ribbons (silver, cream, navy, red) → silver or gold edges depending on contrast desired.
  3. Neutral leathers (brown, tan, cognac) can take almost any ribbon color — use them to test bold combos.
  4. Always include cream. It's the universal bridge color. Every combo should have at least one cream ribbon.
  5. Gold edges pair best with warm leathers. Silver edges with cool leathers. Painted edges (matching the leather) for understated looks.
  6. 3 ribbons is the minimum. 4 is premium. Never 2 — looks cheap. Never 5+ — looks busy.
  7. Matching headbands to ribbon colors ties the whole package together.

Starter Ribbon Order (First 5 Colors)

If you're buying ribbons for the first time, order these 5 Berisford colors — they cover 90% of all combos:

  1. Cream #48 — universal neutral, goes with everything
  2. Gold #10 — premium accent, pairs with warm leathers
  3. Burgundy #22 — classic accent for brown/oxblood/navy
  4. Navy #56 — cool accent for gray/silver/oxblood
  5. Sage #57 — natural accent for brown/tan/green

Buy 5-10 yards of each. Berisford 10mm Double Satin ribbon is the industry standard — used by Resurrection, Byrd Bound, and Redbird. Available on Amazon or direct from Berisford.

Quick Note on Thread

Linen thread 25/3 in natural/ecru is the default for text block sewing — it works with every color scheme because it's hidden inside the signatures. For cover sewing (if visible), match thread to leather color. TalasOnline carries undyed natural linen which is your best all-purpose choice.
