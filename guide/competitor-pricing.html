Key Competitor Details

Rockwall Bibles — Adam Johnson

Site: rockwallbibles.com

Adam Johnson (Rock Wall Bibles) was listed in our source info.txt but was missing from our earlier analysis. He runs a full custom rebinding shop with a strong focus on Italian Badalassi Carlo leathers. His ready-made Bibles start at $315. Offers both cowhide and goatskin options with extensive customization including yapp sizes, custom spine stamping, page dye, and speckling. Notable for using Seidel Double Shot (US-made Milwaukee leather) as an alternative to Italian imports.

Redbird Bibles — Kevin Schaub

Site: redbirdbibles.com/pricing-guide

Transparent pricing on their 2026 guide. Three size tiers ($285/300/325) with Capra Granulosa goatskin included. Cowhide upcharges of $25-30. Redbird Basics at $250-275 is their value tier with limited leather/ribbon options. $100 non-refundable deposit required. Waitlist-based — you pay deposit ~3 months before your build slot.

Byrd Bound — Cael Byrd

Site: byrdbound.com

Starting at $350 with an impressive online customization wizard. Badalassi Carlo cowhide included (Waxy, Pueblo, Minerva). Goatskin options: Sokoto (+$40), Capra Granulosa (+$40), Alran Patnat (+$50), Siegel Levant (+$60). Page dye +$40, speckles +$100, hand stitching +$100. 300+ Bibles rebound, ~10 week lead time. $200 non-refundable deposit.

Resurrection Rebinding — Neil McCauley

Site: resurrectionrebinding.com/pricing

Very comprehensive pricing. Four size tiers ($310/$340/$370/$400). Best value for included features — ALL leather choices included at base price (Badalassi Carlo cowhide, Capra Granulosa, Levant, Sokoto Traditional, Patina Sauvage, Double Shot). Standard includes hand perimeter stitching, lambskin liner, 3 Berisford ribbons, yapp choices, spine stamping. $40 for gilt line or page dye. No longer accepting major restoration work.

Post Tenebras Lux — Jeffrey Rice

Site: ptlbiblerebinding.com/price-menu

$345 base for cowhide, $395-445 for goatskin. Early pioneer in the modern Bible rebinding movement (mentioned in info.txt as one of the first along with Ben Venoy and Diego Calico). Endorsed by James White (Alpha & Omega Ministries). Per-letter pricing for spine/cover stamping.

What YouTube Recommends

Based on the info.txt research (Foundry Bibles channel and broader community):

  • Best base Bibles for rebinding are consistently ESV Heritage, NKJV Wide Margin, KJV Maclaren, NASB Reference, LSB Giant Print, CSB Thinline, NIV Wide Margin, and NLT Filament
  • Most recommended leathers: Goatskin (Morocco/Capra Granulosa/Levant) and Badalassi Carlo Italian cowhide
  • Top rebinders featured in videos: Jacob Bakas (Reformed & Rebound) ranked #1 by Foundry Bibles, with IC/ICA #2, Flourish #3, Redbird #4
  • Key insight: Multiple YouTubers note that the market is supply-constrained. Top rebinders have months-long waitlists. There is room for new entrants who deliver quality.
