Starter Checklist — Order of Priorities

If you're buying in stages, here's the priority order:

  1. Essential — ~$200: VEVOR book press, bone folder, X-Acto + #11 blades, cutting mat, metal ruler, Jade 403 PVA, glue brushes, awl, linen thread, needles, beeswax, wax paper.
  2. Sewing — ~$80: Sewing frame (or DIY for $15), Berisford ribbons, headbands, sandpaper pack, gold acrylic paint (for practice edges).
  3. Materials — ~$80: Calfskin leather, Davey Board, bookbinding cloth, base Bible (buy one to practice on).
  4. Finishing — ~$120: Entry foil stamper, brass type set, foil rolls, skiving knife, sharpening stone.
  5. Gilding — ~$100: Gold leaf, gilding size, agate burnisher, gilding brush, gilding cushion, gilding knife.

Estimated full startup tool investment: $400-600 for functional (able to complete a quality rebind) · $1,200-2,000 for full setup including stamping and gilding.
