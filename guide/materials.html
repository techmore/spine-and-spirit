Materials & Supplies Reference

Comprehensive guide to every material used in the rebinding process — from leather selection to thread types.

Leather Types

Leather Grade Cost/sq ft Per Bible Durability Best For
Goatskin (Morocco) Full Grain $8 - $14 $20 - $35 Excellent Premium rebinds, heirloom quality
Calfskin Full Grain $6 - $10 $15 - $25 Very Good Mid-range and premium rebinds
Sheepskin (Skiver) Full Grain $4 - $7 $10 - $17 Good Entry-level or lightweight rebinds
Lambskin Full Grain $5 - $8 $12 - $20 Good Soft, flexible covers
Recommendation: Start with calfskin for standard rebinds ($15-25 cost) and goatskin for premium ($25-35). These offer the best balance of durability, workability, and customer perception.
