Selection Criteria

  • Sewn binding — Must have sewn signatures, not glued. Glued bindings cannot be rebound.
  • Paper quality — 36 GSM or higher European Bible paper is ideal.
  • Margin width — Wider margins = better finished product and note-taking.
  • Availability — Must be consistently available from Amazon/ChristianBook at stable prices.
  • Popularity — ESV, NIV, KJV, CSB, NKJV are top sellers.
Strategy: Start with ESV Church Bible ($12.90) and NIV Gift Bible ($11.60) for entry-tier. Use ESV Heritage ($18.50), NKJV Wide Margin ($16), CSB Thinline ($12.90) for standard tier. Use KJV Maclaren ($21) and LSB Giant ($22) for premium tier.
