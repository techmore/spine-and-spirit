Selection Criteria

  • Sewn binding — Must have sewn signatures, not glued. Glued bindings cannot be rebound properly.
  • Paper quality — 36 GSM or higher European Bible paper is ideal. Thinner paper is harder to work with.
  • Margin width — Wider margins make for a better finished product and allow for notes.
  • Availability — Must be consistently available from Amazon/ChristianBook at stable prices.
  • Popularity — More popular translations sell better. ESV, NIV, KJV are top sellers.
Strategy: Start with the best value bases (ESV Church $12.90, NIV Gift $11.60) for lower price-point offerings. Use premium bases (KJV Maclaren $21, LSB Giant $22) for premium-tier products.
